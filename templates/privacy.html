1. Who we are
GovtPrep is an AI-powered exam preparation platform for Indian government competitive exams. We are operated by LOVEUAD LTD, registered in England and Wales (Company No. 16838046).
2. What data we collect
Email address and display name when you register.
Practice session data — which topics you attempted, your answers, time taken, and scores.
Payment information — processed by Razorpay. We do not store card numbers or bank details.
Device and browser information for security and debugging.
3. How we use your data
To provide the service — tracking your progress, showing your rank, identifying weak areas.
To improve the platform — understanding which topics are hardest helps us make better questions.
To process payments and prevent fraud.
We do not sell your data to any third party. We do not use your data for advertising.
4. AI and your answers
Your answers are sent to Google Gemini API to generate feedback and explanations. Google's data processing terms apply to this interaction. We do not use your answers to train our own models.
5. Data storage
Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) hosted in the EU. We use industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest.
6. Your rights
You can request a copy of your data, ask us to delete your account, or correct any information. Email us at privacy@govtprep.in and we will respond within 7 days.
7. Cookies
We use a single session cookie to keep you logged in. No advertising or tracking cookies.
8. Contact
For any privacy questions: privacy@govtprep.in
